Pizza Chicken Ranch

Recipe Description

Ingredients

  • Yeast
  • Bread flour
  • Olive oil
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Chicken
  • Ranch Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Tomato sauce

Steps to make pizza dough

  • Add warm water, yeast, and sugar to a bowl. Mix until creamy
  • Then add flour, oil, and salt. Beat until smooth
  • Rest for 5 minutes.
  • Pat it into a circle
  • Oil pizza pan. Put the dough in the pizza pan
  • Put sauce and toppings.
  • Bake in oven
